The Terminal Knot

Abstract: A proposal for a non-algorithmic, distributed index of static hypertext nodes.

The contemporary internet is defined by ephemerality and dynamic generation. The Terminal Knot (TTK) serves as a structural counter-proposal: a rigid, manually curated ring of static artifacts.
