Criteria for Inclusion

The Terminal Knot is not an open directory. It is a curated graph. To attach your node to the ring, you must satisfy the following heuristics:

  • Structural Integrity: The site must be legible without JavaScript. Pure HTML/CSS is the preferred substrate.
  • Aesthetic Rigor: We privilege the esoteric and the high-functioning. Personal blogs, technical documentation, and digital gardens are encouraged.
  • Zero Tracking: Analytics scripts and third-party trackers are grounds for immediate rejection.

To submit, transmit your URL and a brief manifest (plain text) to the administrator.
